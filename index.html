Privacy Policy for Nezaklad Bot

Last Updated: March 23, 2025

Welcome to Nezaklad Bot ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Telegram bot ("Nezaklad Bot") and related services, including posting to Instagram via the Instagram Graph API. By using our bot, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you interact with Nezaklad Bot:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Telegram User Data: When you interact with the bot on Telegram, we collect your Telegram User ID and, if available, your Telegram username.
  • Product Information: When you add a product to be posted, you provide details such as the product name, source, size, price, link, and expiration timer.
  • Photos and Files: You may upload photos or files (e.g., JPEG/PNG images) to be posted on Telegram and Instagram.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Instagram Data: When you authorize our bot to post to Instagram, we collect your Instagram Account ID and an access token to interact with the Instagram Graph API on your behalf.
  • Post IDs: After posting to Telegram or Instagram, we store message IDs (from Telegram) and Instagram Post IDs to manage and update posts (e.g., mark as sold).

1.3 Information Stored in Our Database

We store the following data in our SQLite database ("products.db"):

  • Product details (name, source, size, price, link, expiration timer).
  • Telegram User ID of the user who added the product.
  • Telegram message IDs and Instagram Post IDs associated with the product.
  • Photos (as file IDs) temporarily until the product is posted.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Posting Products: To post product information, including photos, to the Telegram channel (@nezaklad_shop) and your Instagram account via the Instagram Graph API.
  • Managing Products: To manage product listings, including marking products as sold, updating posts, and deleting expired products.
  • Notifications: To notify owners and managers (via Telegram) about product postings, user interest, and purchase requests.
  • Service Operation: To operate and maintain the bot, including storing data in our database for functionality.

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information in the following ways:

  • Telegram: We send product information and photos to Telegram API to post in the @nezaklad_shop channel and to notify owners and managers.
  • Instagram: We send product information and photos to the Instagram Graph API to post on your Instagram account.
  • Service Providers: We do not share your data with third-party service providers, except for Telegram and Instagram as described above.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

4. Data Storage and Retention

We store your data as follows:

  • Photos and Files: Photos and files you upload are stored temporarily on our server as "temp_*.jpg" files and are deleted immediately after posting to Telegram and Instagram.
  • Product Data: Product information is stored in our SQLite database ("products.db") until the product is marked as sold or the expiration timer is reached, at which point the data is deleted.
  • Instagram Access Token: Your Instagram access token is stored securely and used only to interact with the Instagram Graph API on your behalf.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Correction: You can request access to or correction of your data by contacting us (see Section 9).
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data by contacting us. Note that product data is automatically deleted when the product is sold or expires.
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the bot at any time to opt out of data collection.

If you are located in the European Union (EU), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States, where Telegram and Instagram servers are located. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR where applicable.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you via Telegram or by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the bot after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
